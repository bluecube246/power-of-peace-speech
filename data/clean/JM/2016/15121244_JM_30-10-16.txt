News  KC breaks ground for synthetic Byron Bachelor running track  Kingston College on Friday officially broke ground to construct a 300-metre synthetic running track at its Upper Elletson Road venue . The project is to be managed by Pavecon Ltd and is scheduled to be completed by mid-February . It is estimated to cost US$440,000 , of which 75 per cent was donated by the United States-based RuJohn Foundation , which is headed by KC Old Boy Byron Bachelor , after whom the track will be named .
