DKB to storm UK for African comedy festival
http://citifmonline.com/2016/03/18/dkb-to-storm-uk-for-african-comedy-festival/

<h> DKB to storm UK for African comedy festival <p> Ghanaian comedian , DKB has been selected to perform at this year 's African comedy festival in UK . <p> He will be performing alongside comedy heavyweights from other countries at the Copper box arena , Queen Elizabeth Olympic Park London , United Kingdom E20 3HB on Saturday June 25 , 2016 . <p> DKB will share the stage with notable top comics from Nigeria , Kenya , Somalia , South Africa , Ethiopia , Zimbabwe , Uganda among others . <p> The festival night will be preceded days before with a special show for the Ghanaian populace in the UK , which DKB is surely going to tear into pieces . <p> This is the best time to have a memorable experience with non-stop rib cracking comedy brewed in the African pot . <p> The African Comedy festival will be filmed and aired for worldwide audiences across the world . <p> DKB is expected to fly out of Ghana weeks before the event to enable him prepare adequately ahead of time . <p> The comedian said " @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ to be chosen to represent Ghana in the UK . It comes as a dream come true to tour the world with #GHcomedy and this is just the beginning . " <p> " I will entreat all my fans in the UK to grab a ticket for us to have a wonderful night as I showcase to other citizens what Ghanaian talent is made of . I wish to thank all my fans , friends and Ghanaians in general for supporting my career , God truly bless them , " he added .
