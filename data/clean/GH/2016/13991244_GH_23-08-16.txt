10 African youngsters still available for transfer  1 of 10  Amadou Diawara : The Guinean midfielder has already proved his class in Serie A with Bologna , making 34 appearances and an average of 2.2 tackles per match last term , and now appears primed to move to one of the division 's bigger dogs . The 19-year-old recently had a medical at Napoli and a deal is expected soon , although until a transfer is confirmed , reported suitors Juventus and Aston Villa may yet fancy their chances of gazumping the Partenopei.
