Olympics to sign Hearts midfielder Sabahn Laryea
http://www.ghanaweb.com/GhanaHomePage/SportsArchive/Olympics-to-sign-Hearts-midfielder-Sabahn-Laryea-481532

The Hearts of Oak young enforcer is set to join their city rivals on a three-year deal .  According to Kickgh.com sources , the 21-year-old contract with the Rainbow club has run out , and he is not ready to extend his stay .  " He Sabahn Laryea is one of the best players in our local league , so he is one of our targeted players . We will make some signings to help us to stay in the league " a close source to Olympics told Kickgh.com  It is believed that Ebusua Dwarfs and Elmina Sharks have also expressed interest in landing the youngster .
