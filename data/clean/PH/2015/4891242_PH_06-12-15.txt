NBP inmate stabbed dead in spite of strict controls
http://www.interaksyon.com/article/121135/nbp-inmate-stabbed-dead-in-spite-of-strict-controls

manila - despite recent operations under " oplan galugad " conducted by security authorities to ferret out weapons and contraband items , an inmate at the new bilibid prison ( nbp ) in muntinlupa city was stabbed dead in the wee hours monday morning .  the initial report identified the victim as felicito braga , who expired at nbp 's prison hospital where he was brought for treatment after the incident that took place at about 1 a.m. at the maximum security compound 's dorm 12-b .  the assailant was identified as braga 's cousin , jonathan .  an investigation is ongoing to determine the motive for the stabbing incident .  last october 26 , another nbp inmate , antonio usis , was also stabbed by another inmate , alan pinot .  also in october , former masbate jail warden charlie quidato was shot to death inside his cell by another inmate .  quidato was serving a sentence for the killing of masbate mayor moises espinosa , jr . in august 2001 when he was shot dead .
