<h> Hope in a Bottle garners Global Awards <p> Hope in a Bottle garnered two awards at the recently held Global Bottled Water Congress in Lisbon , Portugal on Oct. 21 , 2015 . <p> Hope in a Bottle is the purified water company that donates 100-percent of profits to building public school classrooms . Represented by the founder , Nanette Medved-Po , at the awards , HIB won " Best New Brand " and " Best Community Initiative " at the 12th annual awarding ceremonies organized by beverage industry consultant Zenith International . <p> " I am grateful to the people at Zenith for scouring the globe and finding us . To have an international panel select our effort out of the many very worthy initiatives that have been nominated , honors both the Philippines and the work we do at Hope , " said Medved-Po . <p> Friends of Hope was started in 2012 in an effort to create a citizen movement for the benefit of public education .
