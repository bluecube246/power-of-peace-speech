<h> Universal Robina , Danone form beverage joint venture <p> MANILA -- Universal Robina Corp ( URC ) and the French maker of Evian water and Activia yogurt are forming a joint venture . <p> In a disclosure to the Philippine Stock Exchange , the Gokongwei-led URC said it entered into a deal with Danone Asia Holdings Private Ltd . Both parties will put up Danone Universal Robina Beverages Inc , which will be equally owned by URC and Danone . <p> " Through the joint venture , URC aims to diversify its line of beverage products , increase the sales of its various beverage products and expand its market share in the Philippines , " the listed firm said . The partnership is expected to start commercial operations by next year . <p> Danone Asia manufactures and supplies fresh dairy products , waters , early life nutrition , and medical nutrition in different areas of the world . <p> The joint venture firm will have an initial paid-in capital of approximately P266 million .
