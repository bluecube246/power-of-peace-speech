<h> TransAsia acquires 2 power barges from Napocor <h> TransAsia acquires 2 power barges from Napocor <p> TRANS-Asia Oil and Energy Development Corp . recently acquired P400-million worth of two power barges stationed in Iloilo to expand its services in Visayas . <p> Danielle del Rosario , Trans-Asia head of Corporate Affairs , said the acquisition of the power barges will augment the power needs in Visayas , particularly in Panay and Guimaras islands . <p> Del Rosario said the two power barges , NPC Power Barge 101 based at Bo . Obrero and Power Barge 102 formerly stationed in Estancia , Iloilo , are now stationed in Iloilo City . <p> The barges , carrying 32 megawatts ( MW ) each , will have a combined 64 MW power to be attached to the Panay grid as provided for in the agreement with the Power Sector Asset and Liability Management Corp ( Psalm ) . <p> Trans-Asia reconditioned and repaired the power barges to make it more profitable , environment-friendly and assurance of enough supply of power to meet the growing power demands of provinces and cities @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ of 27 turbines by harnessing wind energy in the municipality of San Lorenzo , Guimaras . <p> The project was completed last year to provide 54 megawatts of wind power and ensure electric power to the whole island of Guimaras distributed by the Guimaras Electric Cooperative . <p> Excess power is connected to the National Grid Corp . for the use of other electric cooperatives in Panay island .
