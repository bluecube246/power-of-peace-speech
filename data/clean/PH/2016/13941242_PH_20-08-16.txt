QC cop busted in extort try

qc cop busted in extort try  a quezon city police officer was arrested friday evening for allegedly trying to extort p100,000 in exchange for a drug suspect 's release .  police officer 1 michael gragasin , 33 , of brgy . kaligayahan , novaliches , was collared in an entrapment operation around 8 p.m. by the quezon city police district - special operations unit ( qcpd-dsou ) at the compound of the masambong police station ( ps-2 ) .  gragasin has been assigned at the ps-2 's station anti-illegal drugs ( said ) since march 15 , 2016 .  qcpd director sr. supt. guillermo eleazar said his arrest came after a certain jackielyn pineda friday afternoon reported to the dsou police that gragasin maltreated her live-in partner zaldy roda who was nabbed for selling illegal drugs .  eleazar said roda earlier that same day was arrested in a buy-bust operation in brgy . paltok . he was brought to ps-2 and turned over to said for filing of appropriate charges .
