llanes : changes have started  llanes : changes have started  the country 's sixteenth chief executive rodrigo roa duterte has exhibited his version of change in his installation as president of our republic by just being simple .  his simple light brown philippine barong made in davao which proudly showed our countries flag is a testament to what he espoused as being a simple filipino who truly loves his country .  and in accordance to his simplicity , he has urged our countrymen to also start the genuine and meaningful change from within . he said this is the fundamental point for the country to start the positive change which the country needs .  in his acceptance speech however , president rody stressed to all of his cabinet members , department heads , and the government to listen to the people and to know how to supply their needs .  president rody pointed out the problem of corruption in government , criminality , illegal drugs , the breakdown of law and order and the judicial system as the main causes for .  it will be his goal to instruct public servants to provide the filipinos a better , safer and healthy society which he said , " all is not lost " .  he stressed his dismay with government offices in bending and changing the rules at the thick of the game . it is the reason why he has called on all departments of government to simplify rules and regulations regarding government contracts .  this he said " will be a test for government on how it will truly serve the filipinos " .  although his journey as the sixteenth president will be rough , he have pledge to serve not one segment of the society , but to serve all the filipinos .  and since he is now ready to start work for the nation , it is the duty of the filipinos to truly show our love for country by showing concern to those who have less and who our helpless .  published in the sun.star baguio newspaper on july 01 , 2016 .  latest issues of sun.star baguio also available on to
