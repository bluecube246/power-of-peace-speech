<h> Off the beaten track <h> In conversation with Anisur Rahman Milon <p> Shah Alam Shazu <p> Anisur Rahman Milon , a popular face on television , has recently signed the contract for a new film , although this is not his first appearance on the big screen . The busy actor recently had a conversation with The Daily Star , on his works and other subjects . <p> Tell us about the TV serial " Nirbikar Manush " . <p> Milon : The story of this drama is a little offbeat . I play the role of an indifferent man , and the story revolves around my character .
