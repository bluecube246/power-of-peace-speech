nitish for ' impartial probe ' into bhopal ' encounter '  patna : a day after rjd chief lalu prasad demanded a high-level probe into the killing of eight undertrial simi men in madhya pradesh , ally jd ( u ) president and bihar cm nitish kumar on wednesday sought an " impartial inquiry by an independent agency " into the " encounter " near bhopal .  " there is a need of more clarity on the incident . the eight undertrial people escaped together and were found together . also , there are videos of the ' encounter ' issued by the authorities . certain questions are coming to the minds of people . if questions are being raised , then it should be probed , " nitish told reporters in patna .  lalu on tuesday said only a high-level probe would clear the suspense whether it was a real or fake encounter .
