<h> Lokayukta takes cognizance of land conversion around Ranthambore park <p> JAIPUR : Rajasthan Lokayukta has taken suo motu cognizance of conversion of land for commercial use based on a factually wrong report by deputy conservator of forests within one km radius of the Ranthambhore national park . <p> The media report said then district collector of Sawai Madhopur , MS Poonam has given permission for the conversion of a piece of land on the periphery of the world famous national park on a factually wrong report by deputy conservator of forests . <p> However , inquiry found that the land is located at 800 meters from the national park not 1100 meters as reported by the forest official . <p> According to the Supreme court order as per Wildlife Protection Act 1972 , all kinds of construction around one km radius of a wildlife sanctuary/national park is prohibited .
