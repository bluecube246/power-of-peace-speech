One more held for attack on Teotia  TNN Aug 25 , 2016 , 01.09 AM IST  Ghaziabad : One more suspect -- a lifer currently out on bail -- has been arrested for his alleged involvement in the attack on local BJP leader Brijpal Teotia even as the main conspirators behind the plot continue to evade arrest .  A joint team of the UP Police 's special task force and the Ghaziabad district police arrested Jogender Rajput alias Babbal ( 35 ) from New Ghaziabad Railway Station at 11.30 pm on Tuesday . A .32 bore semi-automatic pistol , allegedly used by Babbal to fire at Teotia 's vehicle during the attack in Muradnagar , has been recovered from him .  Teotia and his posse of gunmen and aides were travelling in two Scorpios on Rawli Road on August 11 when they were attacked . The assailants following them in two Fortuner SUVs opened fire , including from an AK-47 . All six persons , including Teotia , had survived the attack albeit with serious gunshot injuries .  Cops said Babbal , hired by the main conspirators -- Manish and Manoj -- to eliminate Teotia . He had been convicted in a 2002 murder case in Lajpat Nagar police station area . He was sentenced to life in jail , but is out on bail granted by the Delhi high court .  " Babbal had been involved with Manish and Manoj since the past four months in planning the attack on Teotia . He was amongst those who had opened fire on Teotia . Investigations have revealed that the AK-47 rifle had been brought by Manish , " Ghaziabad SSP KS Emmanuel told TOI .  Teotia 's family members fear another attack  With the main conspirators being still at large , the family members of Teotia , who is still admitted to the Fortis hospital in Noida , have informed Ghaziabad police that they perceive the likelihood of yet another attack on him .  On Tuesday , his family members submitted a clipping of CCTV footage to Ghaziabad police , which purportedly shows two suspicious persons loitering near the hospital room in which Teotia is recuperating .  " Teotia 's room to be allowed inside . However , family members who were attending upon him could not recognise the men . They sent the two men downstairs to the lobby to meet me . However , they were later not to be seen anywhere , " Brijpal Teotia 's younger brother , Vikas , said .  Stay updated on the go with Times of India News App .
