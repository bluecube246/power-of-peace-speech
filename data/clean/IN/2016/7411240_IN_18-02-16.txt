hyderabad drucc seeks new trains  hyderabad : members of the divisional railway users consultative committee ( drucc ) of hyderabad division , sought introduction of new trains , extension of platforms and increasing the reservation quota .  the 58th drucc meeting was held under the chairmanship of aruna singh , divisional railway manager . ramulu naik , mlc , was among the six members who attended the meeting .  aruna singh assured the members that their suggestions and representations would be examined as per the guidelines in force and action taken accordingly , and sought the support of the members for sponsoring amenities at stations under mplad scheme or through the csr route . naik announced 5 lakh fun for providing amenities soon .  aruna spoke on the division 's financial performance in the current financial year up to january and urged members to contribute their mite in educating rail users on the need to maintain cleanliness on railway premises and trains .  the drm said amenities such as automatic ticket-vending machines including card-cum-cash-based atvms , appointment of facilitators for atvms , a second entry from the north side with booking office , extension of fob at nizamabad were among many provided . elaborate arrangements made during godavari pushkarams at basar , introduction of additional trains , augmentation of coaches , stoppages , etc came in for appreciation from the members .  disclaimer : we respect your thoughts and views ! but we need to be judicious while moderating your comments . all the comments will be moderated by the nie editorial . abstain from posting comments that are obscene , defamatory or inflammatory , and do not indulge in personal attacks . try to avoid outside hyperlinks inside the comment . help us delete comments that do not follow these guidelines .
