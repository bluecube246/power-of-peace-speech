jesu ' ( infant jesus )  tnn oct 18 , 2016 , 03:00 ist  colva : the fama was celebrated on monday with pomp and solemnity at the our lady of mercy church in colva . thousands of devotees flocked to the church to venerate ' menino jesu ' ( infant jesus ) .  high mass was celebrated at 5.30am by the parish priest along with six other priests . the statue of menino jesus was brought down from its casket , placed at the altar and carried around the church in a procession before being kept for veneration till late night .  people of all faiths strongly believe the statue or the ring on its fingers has miraculous powers and flock here to seek blessings for bearing children , for cures of various ailments and even to bless marriages .  people , including long queues , to venerate the statue with a ' umo ' ( kiss ) . infants were specially brought for the fama so that they can be blessed .  colva pi braz menezes said that colva police , in tandem with the margao traffic police , diverted the motorcades of dignitaries and delegates attending brics and bimstec through the khareband route and freed the colva route for the festivity . a bomb disposal squad was present at the
