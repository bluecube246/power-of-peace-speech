Thousands of devotees flocked to the church to venerate ' Menino Jesu ' ( Infant Jesus ) <p> TNN Oct 18 , 2016 , 03:00 IST <p> COLVA : The fama was celebrated on Monday with pomp and solemnity at the Our Lady of Mercy Church in Colva . Thousands of devotees flocked to the church to venerate ' Menino Jesu ' ( Infant Jesus ) . <p> High mass was celebrated at 5.30am by the parish priest along with six other priests . The statue of Menino Jesus was brought down from its casket , placed at the altar and carried around the church in a procession before being kept for veneration till late night . <p> People of all faiths strongly believe the statue or the ring on its fingers has miraculous powers and flock here to seek blessings for bearing children , for cures of various ailments and even to bless marriages . <p> People , including @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ long queues , to venerate the statue with a ' umo ' ( kiss ) .
