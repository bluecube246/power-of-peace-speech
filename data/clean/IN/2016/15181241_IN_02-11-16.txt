Farmer murdered for Rs7Lakh , 3 booked  AGRA : Police have registered a case against three persons for allegedly murdering a farmer for Rs seven lakh at Parvati Pura in Bah block . The body of the deceased was found buried in a dry well on the field of one of the accused .  According to station house officer ( SHO ) of Bah , Satyendar Singh Raghav , the body of Lakhan Singh ( 55 ) was found in a dry well . He was missing since October 26 and on October 31 , his family members had lodged a missing complaint , added the SHO .  He further added , " Lakhan Singh had mortgaged two bigah field to the accused Bhupinder Singh for a loan of Rs five lakh . To release this , he sold another bigah field for Rs10 lakh . "  The SHO said that Lakhan Singh had completed the sale formalities on October 26 following which he went missing . He added that some villagers claimed to have spotted the victim and Bhupinder Singh at the added that the victim was carrying around Rs seven lakh when he went missing .  Sources said that the Lakhan Singh 's family members and police started searching him on Bhupinder 's field based on suspicion and dug up a 15-20 feet deep dry well that was covered with a fresh mound of soil . After several hours , police found Lakhan Singh 's body buried in the well .  Talking to TOI , superintendent of police ( Rural ) of Agra ( East ) R P Gupta said that a case has been registered against Bhupinder Singh , Harendra Singh and their father Janak under section 302 of IPC based on a complaint lodged by the victim 's son Prem Singh .
