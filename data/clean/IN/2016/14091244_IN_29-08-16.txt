' Druggie ' held for molesting Manipuri woman in Kalina  Mumbai : The police have arrested a 25-year-old man , a suspected drug addict , for molesting a 19-year-old woman from Manipur in Kalina , Santacruz ( east ) .  Vicky Jadhav ( 25 ) molested the woman around 11.45 pm on August 19 as she was on her way to her rented home from the spa that employed her , said the police . The arrest was made last week .
