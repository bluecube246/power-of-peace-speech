<h> Bill seeks to give national status to biotech centre <p> A bill to set up a regional centre for biotechnology , which would train manpower for the sector , was introduced in Lok Sabha on Tuesday . <p> TNN Mar 16 , 2016 , 05.38 AM IST <p> New Delhi : A bill to set up a regional centre for biotechnology , which would train manpower for the sector , was introduced in Lok Sabha on Tuesday . The government , through an executive order in April 2009 , had established the Regional Centre for Biotechnology Training and Education in Faridabad . With a legislation , the institute would be able give masters and Phd degrees , like the Indian Institute of Science .
