Andhra Pradesh minister 's son Susheel arrested for molestation of teacher  DECCAN CHRONICLE .  Published Mar 6 , 2016 , 1:55 am IST  Updated Mar 6 , 2016 , 9:50 am IST  CCTV footage showed that Susheel 's car had followed the woman for almost 1 km .  Son of Andhra Pradesh minister Ravela Kishore Babu , Susheel , and his driver were arrested on charges of harassment . ( Photo : Twitter )  Hyderabad : The Banjara Hills police , on Sunday morning , arrested the son of Andhra Pradesh social welfare minister Ravela Kishore Babu , Susheel , who was booked for outraging the modesty of a woman a day before .  Susheel , who surrendered at Banjara Hills Police Station in early morning hours will be produced before the Magistrate later today .  Susheel and his driver had followed a woman pedestrian in their car at Banjara Hills on Friday and attempted to pull her into the vehicle . After she escaped and raised an alarm , the duo was beaten up by a crowd . the woman for almost 1 km . The police had booked only the driver under the pretext that the other person in the car had not been identified .  After a day-long outcry against their alleged inaction , police recorded the statement of the victim , a teacher , and issued notices to Susheel and driver M. Ramesh alias Appa Rao to appear for investigation .
