death in mall : police seek 14-day remand of 4 accused  police on monday produced four friends of gaurang bobde , who have been charged for culpable homicide not amounting to murder in connection with his mysterious death in a city mall here on july 22 .  tnn aug 9 , 2016 , 12.46 pm ist  bilaspur : police on monday produced four friends of gaurang bobde , who have been charged for culpable homicide not amounting to murder in connection with his mysterious death in a city mall here on july 22 .  police asked for a 14-day judicial remand for the accused .  since none of their families presented any bail application , the accused were sent back to jail . the matter would be heard next on august 22 .  four friends of the deceased , ankit malhotra , kinghshuk agrawal , karan khushlani and karan jaiswal , were brought to the district court premises under tight security .  the four were produced before the court of cjm vivek tiwari . apart from family members of the present in the court . in the meanwhile , police said that investigations are on . police have sent the hard disk and other footage collected from the mall to hyderabad for forensic testing . gaurang 's family , including his father , mother and sister had visited civil lines police station and asked for copies of the postmortem report . they were allowed to take pictures of the same on their mobiles .  on july 22 , gaurang and his friends had partied till late night at a pub in a city mall .  the next morning , gaurang 's body with multiple injuries was found in the basement of the mall . while none of his friends were with him , they also did not inform his family about the mishap .  he was taken to hospital by guards of the mall , where he was declared brought dead . following gaurang 's death , a number of agitations and protests were held over failure of the police to arrest the accused . gaurang 's family alleged that it was a murder and demanded a cbi other hand , kept impressing that it was not a murder .  they even reconstructed the event with a dummy and declared that the incident was an accident . after a lot of hue and cry was raised over police investigations , police rounded up four of his friends and charged them under part two of section 304 ipc ( culpable murder not amounting to homicide ) . senior police officials , including ig bilaspur range vivekanand sinha , have
