" the first book swap meet organised a couple of months ago received lukewarm response in the city but things started changing slowly and the recent meet-ups turned out well where many youngsters came on board with us . in the light of the recent release of a latest book on harry potter , book lovers discussed the book at the meet with a cuppa and it was a sheer joy where all of us were totally engrossed in discussions without the distraction of smartphones . looking at the great success of these meet-up , we have made it a monthly event , " said one of the organisers of the meet , sukhada chaudhary .  inspires young writers  these book swap meets serve as a great platform for young writers to develop their creative and communication skills . a young writer , chaitali bangre , shared : " young writers in city often do n't get a literary platform where they can share their knowledge and creativity , but with book swap meet-ups catching platform where they also get to know what young readers like to read . "  expand your worldview  these meet-ups are not only evolving book reading habits among the youngsters in the city , but they also help expand their worldview . " i am glad that youngsters are investing their time to read classic books and indulging in literary debates . watching television or playing games on smartphones must not be discouraged , but one must balance it all and invest some time exploring the finer nuances of literature too . books are not only a great companion for anyone wanting to discover the view of a writer , but they expand one 's worldview and nurture critical thinking too , " said litterateur dr tapati dey .  sharing moments of fun  interestingly , these rendezvous are also a great way for youngsters to share some lighter moments along with intelligent conversation .
