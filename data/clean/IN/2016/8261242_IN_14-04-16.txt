<h> Incestuous relationship between cousins leads to murder of a youth in Mainpuri <p> AGRA : An alleged incestuous relationship between two cousins from Dalit families led to the murder of a 22-year old youth in Mainpuri district late night on Wednesday . <p> According to police the youth , Sanjay , a student of local ITI college , was having an affair with his maternal cousin sister and when the girl 's parent found out about their relationship , they were infuriated and decided to kill him . Sanjay was killed in a literal execution style , with the three accused , all related to the girl , putting a country made pistol on the right side of his head and shooting him at point blank range . <p> The police have arrested the girl 's father , Ramratan ( 42 ) , her brother , Shailender ( 23 ) , and another relative , Shri Krishan ( 38 ) on charges of murder . Police officials said the murder took place on Wednesday night in Village Ghiror at about 8 PM .
