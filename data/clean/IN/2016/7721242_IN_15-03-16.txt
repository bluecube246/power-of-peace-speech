' Doctors faced action over foeticide '  In the drive against female foeticide , the government has taken action against several medical professionals convicted under the PC &amp; PNDT Act , the government informed the Rajya Sabha on Tuesday .  TNN Mar 16 , 2016 , 05.36 AM IST  New Delhi : In the drive against female foeticide , the government has taken action against several medical professionals convicted under the PC &amp; PNDT Act , the government informed the Rajya Sabha on Tuesday .  Various state governments have filed 2,152 cases , secured 306 convictions and sealed and seized 1,573 ultrasound machines for violation of the Act , which prohibits female foeticide .  Health minister J P Nadda said the registrations of 100 medical professionals convicted under the Act were suspended or cancelled by state medical councils concerned . " Doctors are acted upon and action is taken against family members also , " Nadda said , adding corrective action was being taken to improve the sex ratio .
