<h> Chit-fund co dupes Bhilai residents to the tune of crores <p> A case of fraud has been registered against Togo Swasthya Evam Jan Kalyan Sansthan ( TSEJKS ) , an Uttar Pradesh based chit-fund company accused of duping its customers in Chhattisgarh to the tune of over Rs 100 crore . <p> TNN Apr 9 , 2016 , 12.45 PM IST <p> Bhilai : A case of fraud has been registered against Togo Swasthya Evam Jan Kalyan Sansthan ( TSEJKS ) , an Uttar Pradesh based chit-fund company accused of duping its customers in Chhattisgarh to the tune of over Rs 100 crore . <p> The incident has exposed police laxity in detecting fraudulent chit fund companies still operating in state . Based on numerous complaints by depositors , police have registered a case against six officials . <p> Police said that the company had defaulted on interest payments to investors . Six officials , including TSEJKS director Prithvi Singh Sethi , general managers Ajay Singh , AK Mandal , regional head Prashant Majumdar , area manager Prashant Majumdar and branch manager PK Das have been named @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ A case under Sections 409 , 420 and 34 of IPC has been registered in this connection , and we are further looking into the matter , " said Chhaoni station in charge Rakesh Joshi . <p> The company , through its various investment schemes , like MIS and other modes , lured petitioners to make investments in it and other sister companies . Investment to the tune of around Rs 150 crore has been done in this company . Allegedly , the company has fled and its local office is locked . <p> The matter came to light last week , after 44 people belonging to Bhilai , Durg and Rajnandgaon , who had invested in TSEJKS , filed a petition in high court urging seizure of properties and assets of company officials .
