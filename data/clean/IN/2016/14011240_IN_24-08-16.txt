<h> Woman shot dead in ' robbery bid ' <p> TNN Aug 25 , 2016 , 01.12 AM IST <p> Ghaziabad : A 30-year-old woman was killed in her house in Loni 's Naveen Kunj on Wednesday morning . The victim , Nishaira , wife of a cable operator , was allegedly shot dead by robbers , her husband , Usman Ali , claimed . Police , however , suspect enmity to be the reason behind the murder . <p> Anil Kumar Yadav , DSP , said , " We received a call from the victim 's husband at 9.45am saying his wife was shot in her house and that she was being taken to GTB Hospital in Delhi . Later we came to know that Nishaira had succumbed to her injuries on the way . When we questioned the neigbours we came to know that she was killed while resisting a robbery bid . Nothing was missing from the house , though .
