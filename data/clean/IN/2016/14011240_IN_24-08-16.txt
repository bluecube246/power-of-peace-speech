Woman shot dead in ' robbery bid '  TNN Aug 25 , 2016 , 01.12 AM IST  Ghaziabad : A 30-year-old woman was killed in her house in Loni 's Naveen Kunj on Wednesday morning . The victim , Nishaira , wife of a cable operator , was allegedly shot dead by robbers , her husband , Usman Ali , claimed . Police , however , suspect enmity to be the reason behind the murder .  Anil Kumar Yadav , DSP , said , " We received a call from the victim 's husband at 9.45am saying his wife was shot in her house and that she was being taken to GTB Hospital in Delhi . Later we came to know that Nishaira had succumbed to her injuries on the way . When we questioned the neigbours we came to know that she was killed while resisting a robbery bid . Nothing was missing from the house , though . "  Usman Ali , the woman 's husband , told the police that he had received death theats from local goons in the Ali 's fourth wife and the couple has a two-and-a-half-month-old son . " His first wife , Sitara , was found dead under mysterious circumstances seven years ago . He divorced his second and third wives , " a neighbour said , adding , " With such a history there is definitely something fishy in the murder of Nishaira .
