The 17-bank consortium , led by State Bank of India , had in March tried to sell this flagship property here of the liquor baron Vijay Mallya in a bid to recover part of the dues . The auction was a no-show as bidders stayed away citing high reserve price of Rs 150 crore . <p> " The reserve price is Rs 135 crore below which the property will not be sold , " according to a public notice put up by SBI Caps , which is auctioning the property on behalf of the lenders . @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ sq ft in the plush Vile Parle area near domestic airport , will be auctioned on August 8 .
