The dacoits attacked the duo with axe-like weapon . <p> Ale police station has identified the deceased couple as Shankar Bhimaji Pansare ( 45 ) and his wife Sangeeta ( 40 ) , both residents of Pansaremala in Sakore village . The couple stayed in a secluded house near their farm . <p> Police said the suspects had entered their house around midnight and stole valuables . The couple woke up to the sound of the suspects leaving .
