Reliance Jio hit back , saying the charge is a bid to block its full rollout . <p> RJio has also demanded the Telecom Regulatory Authority of India act against dominant players , including Airtel , for denying it interconnection in " breach " of the licence agreement . <p> The COAI , @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ , said they have provided RJio sufficient capacity to support 10 times more customer base than it currently claims .
