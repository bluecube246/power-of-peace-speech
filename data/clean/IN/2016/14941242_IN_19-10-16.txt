<h> Tribunal confines re-allocation of Krishna water to Telangana , AP <p> IANS Oct 19,2016 , 04:22 PM IST <p> Krishna Water <p> Krishna Water <p> ? New Delhi/Hyderabad : In a setback to Telangana and Andhra Pradesh , the Krishna Water Disputes Tribunal-II on Wednesday ruled that there was no need to re-allocate the river water among all four riparian states . <p> The much-awaited verdict has come as a relief to Maharashtra and Karnataka , which were opposing re-allocation among all states . <p> The tribunal said the water should be re-allocated between Telangana and Andhra Pradesh , the two successor states of undivided Andhra Pradesh . <p> It asked the Telugu states to submit their objections and arguments , if any , within four weeks . The tribunal adjourned the next hearing till December 14 . <p> Telangana Advocate General Ramakrishna Reddy said the tribunal order was not final and was subject to a review by the Supreme Court . <p> He told reporters that they had sought re-allocation of the river water among four states as per Section 89 of the Andhra Pradesh Reorganisation @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ that the dispute was confined to only the Telugu states . <p> Both Andhra Pradesh and Telangana had sought fresh allocation among all four riparian states . <p> On the other hand , Maharashtra and Karnakata had argued that Andhra Pradesh Reorganisation Act , 2014 was limited to successor states of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana and Maharashtra and Karnataka were not parties to it . <p> They wanted the tribunal to re-allocate water to Telugu states from the share of undivided Andhra Pradesh . <p> In 2013 , the tribunal had made allocation of water among Maharashtra , Karnataka and undivided Andhra Pradesh . <p> However , Andhra Pradesh had challenged the award on the ground that injustice was done to it . After the formation of Telangana , the state also impleaded in the case .
