" this was our last practice match against australia and now we are all prepared to give a much better performance in the upcoming tournament . after winning the match against australia , we have gained a lot of confidence and hope that we end the tournament on a positive note , " he said .  australian coach graham reid praised the indians but insisted that his side would bounce back .
