<p> MUMBAI:OMRON Healthcare India , a key player in the health care segment providing innovative devices for monitoring vitals - has introduced OMRON VP-1000 Plus . It is a non-invasive vascular screening monitor for the prevention of cardiovascular diseases . <p> The company is one of the key players in the home monitoring segment . Based on advanced bio information sensing technology , OMRON VP-1000 has numerous features including the TBI package which makes it unique in the market . It brings together the techniques of blood pressure monitoring and pulse wave measurements to act as a communication platform between human body and medical professionals so that they are able to monitor and treat cardiovascular patients in a reliable , accurate , effective and a well-timed manner . <p> " With a rapid increase in number of cardiovascular patients in India , I feel that there is an urgent need of innovative equipment like OMRON VP-1000 Plus . We strive to bring to India newer technologies to help the hospitals , clinics and medical institutes build effective infrastructure so that they are able to provide better solutions to @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ . Vascular scanners are being globally used in medical facilities with numerous clinical validations , " said Shinya Tomoda , MD , Omron Healthcare India . <p> The device provides easy and quantitative assessment of vascular conditions of human body . It uses a wide array of functions to providecomprehensive user-specific reports suiting different needs and purposes . For example , for patients the reports have easy-to -follow illustrations of the heart and the blood vessels ; for doctors , the reports consist of lots of values indicating the pathological conditions . This is very helpful in improving patients ' response to medications . Considering the importance of user interface and portability , the device comes with a touch panel .
