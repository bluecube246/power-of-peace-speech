Center clears widening of Ambala-Kaithal road  Haryana Public Works ( Building and Roads ) Minister , Randeep Singh Surjewala said that National Highway Authority of India has given approval of four laning of National Highway 65 from Ambala to Kaithal .  TNN Feb 10 , 2014 , 07.02 PM IST  CHANDIGARH : Haryana Public Works ( Building and Roads ) Minister , Randeep Singh Surjewala said that National Highway Authority of India has given approval of four laning of National Highway 65 from Ambala to Kaithal . A sum of Rs 724 crore would be spent on four laning of this 96 kilometer long road . Central Government has invited tenders for this big project and its work would start soon .  Surjewala said that this project has been approved by Central Government under National Highway Development Programme .  He said that Kaithal would emerge as new commercial centre with such big projects and with its being set up as main centre , new dimensions of economic development would be set up .  He said that Section 3A has been implemented and most of the area has been informed about it . This project is being made on BOT Toll Model .  He said that under this project , four new bypasses have been approved including Kaithal bypass , Pehowa bypass , Ismailabad bypass and Ambala bypass . All bypasses would be set up by following modern standards .
