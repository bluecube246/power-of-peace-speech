Kinetic Group, Ikaros of Belgium sign solar energy JV

<h> Kinetic Group , Ikaros of Belgium sign solar energy JV <p> Pune-based Kinetic Group has entered into a joint venture with Belgium based Ikaros Solar Group , a company that operates in the solar energy space . <p> Kinetic owns 25 per cent stake in Ikaros Kinetic Solar Pvt Limited , while Ikaros owns 51 per cent and Intelux India , a Pune-based company specialized in power electronics owns the remaining 24 per cent . <p> The JV company plans to provide customized solutions to low capacity solar photovoltaic installations ranging from 20KWp to 1MWp . The focus will be residential and commercial rooftops installations in malls , hotels , resorts , hospitals and industrial sheds amongst others . <p> It has so far installed multiple roof top residential projects in Northern India and is also executing two EPC contracts -- one a 10 MW solar energy project in Gujarat and a 1MW project in Tamil Nadu . It plans to deliver projects of 500 MW in the next five years and touch a top line of Rs 600 crore by then . <p> For Kinetic , @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ " We have keen interest in diversifying businesses related to green energy and this is a step in that direction , " Sulajja Firodia Motwani , vice chairperson , Kinetic Energy Limited , said . <p> The other initiatives under Group company Kinetic Green Energy Power Solutions include building battery operated vehicles ranging from two seats to 14 seats in size . <p> The company has an order for 30 vehicles which being built in KEL 's plant in Ahmednagar , and has delivered the first two , Motwani added .
