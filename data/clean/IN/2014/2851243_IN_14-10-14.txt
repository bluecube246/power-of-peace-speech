Vadodara 's Parul Institute emerges overall champion at youth festival  The two diploma colleges Parul Polytechnic Institute ( PPI ) as well as Parul Institute of Engineering and Technology ( Diploma Studies ) won 23 trophies in all .  TNN Oct 14 , 2014 , 10.48 PM IST  VADODARA : Students from the Parul Group of Institutes from the city emerged overall champions at the state-level youth festival Xitij 2014-15 organised by Gujarat Technological University for diploma engineering institutes in the state .  The two diploma colleges Parul Polytechnic Institute ( PPI ) as well as Parul Institute of Engineering and Technology ( Diploma Studies ) won 23 trophies in all . With 17 trophies in its kitty PPI was declared the overall winner in the state . PPI lead by a handsome margin of 20 points over its nearest competitor .  Over 520 students from 17 diploma engineering colleges across Gujarat participated in Xitij 2014-15 . The event was held at Tolani F G Polytechnic at Adipur in Kutch .
