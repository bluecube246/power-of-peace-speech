Ram ups the style quotient for Pandaga Chesko  Here 's an exclusive dekko at the just released first look of actor Ram from his upcoming film , Pandaga Chesko . From the looks of it -- be it the spiked hair , the designer pants , shoes or the sunglasses and boot -- the actor seems to have upped the style stakes in the movie . Director Gopichand Mallineni informs this movie will see the actor in his stylish best . " Ram has boundless energy and his character in the movie will bring out that live-wire side of him . It 's a role that is tailor made for him , " says the director .
