<p> It was a dream come true for Meghaa Banerjee , a small town girl in West Bengal , India when she earned a scholarship from the prestigious University of New South Wales in Australia to pursue her Masters degree in Economics with inevitable butterflies in her stomach . She embarks upon her maiden expedition to Sydney leaving her protective shell and family behind . <p> Coming from a conservative middle class background , life and culture in Sydney was an instant eye opener for her . Under guidance of her caring cousin Kalpana , Meghaa slowly embraces her new life in Sydney where she makes new set of friends , which includes cherubic Lubaina , prankster Raj , and narcissistic Suhail . <p> Love and romance was something that was strictly not in her agenda of things . However being young at heart it was just something waiting to happen to her when she met Rohit Khurana ( Sharad ) a charismatic , full of sheen and friendly natured fellow student in the University with whom she starts gelling right from the word go .
