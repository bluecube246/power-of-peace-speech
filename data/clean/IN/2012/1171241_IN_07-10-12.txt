<h> Blood donation camp organized in city <p> On Saturday , members of Muslim community joined hands with Hindus for a noble cause during a unique blood donation camp . <p> TNN Oct 7 , 2012 , 11.07 PM IST <p> VADODARA : On Saturday , members of Muslim community joined hands with Hindus for a noble cause during a unique blood donation camp . The camp was organized as a part of Shraddh Paksha ( 15 days during which Hindus do puja to repay debt to their ancestors ) . In Hindu community , it is considered holy to donate something during the Shraddh Paksha and hence the blood donation camp was organized at Gujarat Public School ( GPS ) . <p> " Shraddh Paksha is n't a Muslim ritual , but still many of them turned up to donate blood during the camp . It was a great gesture from them and it proved that humanity still scores above everything in our country , " said Dr R B Bhesania , who runs a blood bank . In Ahmedabad , the awareness about blood donation is far better . Doctors in Vadodara should now take initiative through such innovative ideas , " Nanda told TOI . The camp was attended by city police commissioner Satish Sharma , municipal commissioner Ashwini Kumar and Bakulesh Gupta who runs GPS . <p> About 45 blood donations camps have been organized in the city as a part of Shraddh Paksha wherein about 2,300 donated blood .
