filmmakers to celebrate indian cinema 100 year glory in 2013  in 1913 , when phalke 's pioneering personal cinematography gave magical movement to indian mythology , he gave birth to the country 's biggest passion - cinema . in 2013 , the passion completes its glorious 100 years and indian filmmakers are planning a grand bash for the year .  the indian film and television directors association ( iftda ) - the unique body of film and television directors , a conglomerate of film makers , has taken this initiative to celebrate the centenary year of indian cinema .  as a precursor to the centenary year celebrations , the executive committee of iftda will organize events , shows , retrospectives and other activities showcasing the 100 years of indian cinema panning across the whole of next year i.e. 2012 .  the most senior and respected member of iftda shri . yash chopra will be the guiding light and face of the celebration of indian cinema . alongwith him , stalwarts like shri manmohan shetty , shri . randhir kapoor , shri . mahesh  ashoke pandit , senior vice president of iftda is leading the process which will envision and give direction to the entire project , along with the executive committee members goldie behl , vinta nanda and others .  iftda was established in 1959 by a group of visionaries who have given indian cinema some of its best films in the last century . today , 52 years after establishment , iftda stands tall with a membership of 8000 members and still growing .
