Indian-origin , have found . <p> The addition of a single broadband provider led to as much as a 20% rise in racial hate crimes in areas where racial tensions were especially high , researchers said . <p> The study , the first of its kind to document the relationship between the internet and hate crimes , sourced data from the Federal Bureau of Investigation , the Federal Communications Commission
