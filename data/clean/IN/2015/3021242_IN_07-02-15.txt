cloud computing beneficial : experts  with rapid advancements in the field of information and communication technology ( ict ) , cloud computing has assumed much significance in providing better insights on potential services , innovations and people 's requirements and improving our lifestyles .  tnn feb 7 , 2015 , 11.53 pm ist  patna : with rapid advancements in the field of information and communication technology ( ict ) , cloud computing has assumed much significance in providing better insights on potential services , innovations and people 's requirements and improving our lifestyles . cloud computing technology is fast becoming a lifeline for most developing countries , so far as their socio-economic development is concerned .  these observations were made by ict experts and academia at a daylong seminar on " cloud computing : a paradigm shift in ict " organized by the mca programme of patna university statistics department in collaboration with the computer society of india at geology auditorium here on saturday .  they pointed out that educational institutions have been quick to realize the advantages of cloud computing technology ability of the students to access data anywhere , anytime , to enrol in online classes and to participate in group activities . access is now instantly available and in many instances free , thanks to the proliferation of websites dispensing educational material and cloud knowledge-sharing communities . a simple internet connection can go a long way , they said .  inaugurating the seminar , pu pro-vice-chancellor r k verma highlighted the benefits of cloud computing and pleaded for its wide application for dissemination of knowledge . this technology can prove much beneficial for the poor and marginalized sections of the society , he said .  pu 's former vice-chancellor and central university of bihar ( cub ) ' s statistics department head arun kumar sinha said cloud computing is beneficial not only for the educationists but also for banking , agriculture , health and science . in fact , this technology has revolutionized the entire information technology sector , he said .  computer society of india chairman a k nayak said ict is changing rapidly by adopting faster , effective and latest computing technologies and contributing significantly , people are rapidly adopting mobile phones , internet access and broadband connectivity . in the days to come , there shall be a significant increase in expenditure on it hardware , software and services , he said .  more than 150 delegates , including students and faculty members from patna women 's college , magadh mahila college , st. xavier 's college , indira gandhi national open university , nit patna and central
