<h> Cloud computing beneficial : Experts <p> With rapid advancements in the field of information and communication technology ( ICT ) , cloud computing has assumed much significance in providing better insights on potential services , innovations and people 's requirements and improving our lifestyles . <p> TNN Feb 7 , 2015 , 11.53 PM IST <p> PATNA : With rapid advancements in the field of information and communication technology ( ICT ) , cloud computing has assumed much significance in providing better insights on potential services , innovations and people 's requirements and improving our lifestyles . Cloud computing technology is fast becoming a lifeline for most developing countries , so far as their socio-economic development is concerned . <p> These observations were made by ICT experts and academia at a daylong seminar on " Cloud computing : a paradigm shift in ICT " organized by the MCA programme of Patna University statistics department in collaboration with the Computer Society of India at geology auditorium here on Saturday . <p> They pointed out that educational institutions have been quick to realize the advantages of cloud computing technology @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ ability of the students to access data anywhere , anytime , to enrol in online classes and to participate in group activities . Access is now instantly available and in many instances free , thanks to the proliferation of websites dispensing educational material and cloud knowledge-sharing communities . A simple Internet connection can go a long way , they said . <p> Inaugurating the seminar , PU pro-vice-chancellor R K Verma highlighted the benefits of cloud computing and pleaded for its wide application for dissemination of knowledge . This technology can prove much beneficial for the poor and marginalized sections of the society , he said . <p> PU 's former vice-chancellor and Central University of Bihar ( CUB ) ' s statistics department head Arun Kumar Sinha said cloud computing is beneficial not only for the educationists but also for banking , agriculture , health and science . In fact , this technology has revolutionized the entire information technology sector , he said . <p> Computer Society of India chairman A K Nayak said ICT is changing rapidly by adopting faster , effective and latest computing technologies and contributing significantly @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ , people are rapidly adopting mobile phones , Internet access and broadband connectivity .
