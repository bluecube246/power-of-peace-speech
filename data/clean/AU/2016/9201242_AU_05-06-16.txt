" jason and steve have vast experience in running sporting franchises in america , " said swansea on their webstie .  " jason is managing general owner of mls side , dc united while steve is the executive vice-chairman of the memphis grizzlies in the nba . " all parties expect the takeover to be fully sanctioned by the premier league in the coming weeks .
