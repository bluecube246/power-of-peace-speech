Garissa, Embu come out top in health delivery in Kenya - survey
http://www.the-star.co.ke/news/2016/10/05/garissa-embu-come-out-top-in-health-delivery-in-kenya-survey_c1432785

<p> Garissa and Embu counties have the best health services in Kenya , an annual assessment by the Health ministry shows . <p> The two beat the other 45 counties because of their latest innovations to improve drugs supply , and for reducing maternal deaths by encouraging more poor women to deliver in health facilities . <p> Garissa was rated the best performing county , while Embu came second . <p> All counties , parastatals and health departments had submitted reports on their best practices to the ministry for the annual performance review . <p> " The Ministry of Health has developed criteria for selection of best practices governed by various attributes of the best practice such as its efficiency , cost- effectiveness , community involvement and sustainability , " said Health CS Cleopa Mailu on Wednesday . <p> Mailu said Garissa encourages more poor women to give birth in health facilities through the maternal shelters . <p> Mothers from far-flung areas live in these shelters as they await delivery and for more days after giving birth . <p> They also get the ' Mama Kit ' , @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ also gives traditional birth attendants and community health extension workers Sh200 for every mother they take to a health facility for delivery . <p> The attendants are present during delivery for moral support while in some occasions , they can assist the skilled worker . <p> Embu county was awarded mainly for its successful WhatsApp County Commodity Committee that ensures all the sub-county health facilities have enough supplies all the time . <p> " Innovativeness and inventiveness is underscored in Embu county where the WhatsApp mobile phone application is utilised in pharmaceutical commodity management , " the ministry said . <p> Area Governor Martin Wambora said his government has constructed and fully equipped many dispensaries and upgraded health centres throughout the county . <p> " The health of our people is not negotiable .
