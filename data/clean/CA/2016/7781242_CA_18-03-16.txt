<h> Section of Anderson Road closed during culvert replacement <p> Anderson Road is closed between Ridge Road and Russell Road due to the rehabilitation and construction of a culvert in the area . The work is expected to last until approximately March 23 . Further updates will follow . <p> Road closure and detour signs will be installed and maintained throughout the construction period . Motoriststraveling north and south on Anderson Road are being detoured via Russell Road , Ramsayville Road and Ridge Road . <p> Residents are encouraged to be flexible with commuting times , stagger work hours or use alternative means for travel .
