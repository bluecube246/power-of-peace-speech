<h> Police shooting of armed musician heads to grand jury <h> Poll <p> WEST PALM BEACH , Fla. -- Prosecutors say they 're sending to the grand jury the case of a former police officer who fatally shot a legally armed man who was awaiting a tow truck . <p> Palm Beach County State Attorney Dave Aronberg announced during a news conference Wednesday that his office would continue its investigation of former Palm Beach Gardens officer Nouman Raja in the shooting death of musician Corey Jones . <p> Jones had left a gig before dawn Oct. 18 when his SUV broke down . He was standing nearby when Raja stopped in an unmarked car . Raja was on-duty but not in uniform . <p> Jones had a concealed weapons permit . He and Raja pulled their guns , and Raja shot Jones . <p> The department fired Raja after the shooting . Jones was black while Raja is of South Asian ancestry .
