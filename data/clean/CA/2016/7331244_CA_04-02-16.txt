SQ uses spike strip to end chase with speeding driver on Highway 20
http://montrealgazette.com/news/local-news/sq-uses-spike-strip-to-end-chase-of-speeding-driver-on-highway-20

A police chase on Highway 20 ended with blown tires and an arrest in Vaudreuil-Dorion on Feb. 3 .  S ? ret ? du Qu ? bec Sergeant Bruno Beaulieu said they were alerted by Ontario police at around 7:45 p.m. to watch for a driver who had refused to stop on Highway 20 in Ontario after officers gave chase because he was driving over the speed limit .  SQ officers located the car driving east on Highway 20 at around 8 p.m. and gave chase . A spike mat was laid across the highway a few kilometres from the first sighting , blowing the driver 's tires and bringing the car to a halt on Harwood Blvd. in Vaudreuil-Dorion .  A 28-year-old man from the Toronto region was arrested and faces charges for dangerous driving , failure to stop , refusing a breathalyzer test , driving under the influence and possession of false documents . He was schedule to appear at the Valleyfield courthouse Feb. 4 .
