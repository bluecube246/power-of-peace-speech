High-risk offender eyeing up plea deal
http://www.kelowna.com/2010/02/21/high-risk-offender-eyeing-up-plea-deal/

<h> Court <h> High-risk offender eyeing up plea deal <p> The high-risk violent offender who was at the centre of a high-profile police manhunt in November may bring the saga to a close with guilty pleas . <p> Michael Burwell 's lawyer , Bibhas Vaze told a judge by phone Friday morning in Kelowna provincial court that he is fine-tuning a plea deal with the Crown prosecutor , and the matter was then adjourned to fix a date for sentencing . <p> Burwell faces eight charges , including assault with a weapon , uttering threats and flight from a police officer . <p> This entry was posted on Sunday , February 21st , 2010 at 9:10 am and is filed under Court .
