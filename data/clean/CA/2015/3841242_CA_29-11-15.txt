Movember fundraiser droops as moustache trend wanes
http://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/montreal/movember-fundraiser-decline-1.3342534

<p> Organizers say the men 's health fundraising campaign reached its peak three or four years ago . <p> In 2012 , it raised $42.4 million , according to records filed with the Canada Revenue Agency . Last year it raised barely over half that -- $23.3 million . <p> This year , the Movember Foundation had raised only $12.9 million by Nov. 29 . <h> The short life of a fundraising idea <p> Normand Bourgault , a marketing professor at the University of Quebec in Outaouais , says these kinds of campaigns do n't have a lot of staying power . <p> " The life cycle of a fundraising idea , of a fashionable idea , is quite short : two , three or four years , " he said . <p> " After that , you have to come up with a new idea or new concept that adds something to the original idea . " <p> Bourgault drew a parallel between Movember and the Ice Bucket Challenge , which raised millions for ALS research after sweeping social media last year .
