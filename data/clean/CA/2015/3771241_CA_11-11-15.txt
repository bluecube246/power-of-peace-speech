<h> Twitter 's shift from stars to hearts spikes use of button by six per cent <h> The simple button change divided social media users when it was unveiled last week <p> Twitter changed its starred ' favourites ' button to a heart-shaped ' likes ' button last week . Despite initial outcry over the change , a Twitter employee confirmed that use of the button has spiked six per cent globally since the switch was made .
