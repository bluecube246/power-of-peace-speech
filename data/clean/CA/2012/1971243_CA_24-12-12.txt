East Toronto skating rinks open over the holidays  East Toronto skating rinks open over the holidays  Holiday skating schedules are in effect at outdoor and indoor rinks across the area until the New Year .  East York Memorial Arena will be open to skaters on Thurs . Dec. 27 from 1:15-3:00 p.m .  Following a one day break , the arena re-opens to the public all weekend from 11:30 a.m. to 1 : 30 p.m . After New Year 's Day , the holiday schedule resumes every day from 11:30 to 1:30 p.m. until Sun . Jan. 6 .  East York Memorial Arena is located 888 Cosburn Ave .  At Angela James Arena in Flemingdon Park , public skating will be possible almost every day until Jan. 4 , with the exception of Christmas and New Year 's Day .  On Saturdays , skate times will run from 7:30 p.m. to 9:30 p.m. and 1:00 p.m. to 3:00 p.m. during the week and Sunday Dec. 30 .  The Dieppe Park outdoor complex will offer unsupervised skating and shinny on p.m . Holiday skating will also be available at the double-pad rink , located at 455 Cosburn Ave , from 9 a.m. to 9 p.m. beginning Dec. 27  From Dec. 27 until Jan. 6 , including New Year 's Day , Evergreen Brickworks will open its outdoor ice rink to the public from 11 a.m. to 4 p.m. weather permitting .  Limited skate rentals will also be available , with a suggested donation of five dollars . Children under the age of 12 must be supervised by an adult . For more information visit www.evergreen.ca  For a full list of city-operated skating sites open over the holidays and beyond visit www.toronto.ca
