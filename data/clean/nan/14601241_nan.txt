


Om Namo Venkatesaya has fetched a whopping amount from the sale of its global distribution rights and it is said to be the highest price for the theatrical rights of a film featuring Akkineni Nagarjuna .  The buzz on social media claims that the makers of Om Namo Venkatesaya have already sold its distribution rights in Andhra Pradesh , Telangana ( AP/T ) and international markets . Distributor Dil Raju is said to have bagged its Nizam rights for a record price . The movie is said to have earned Rs 26.50 crore from the sale of its AP/T theatrical rights .  Soggade Chinni Nayana earned Rs 16.60 crore from its AP/T distribution rights and it was the highest amount for a Nagarjuna film . His next release Oopiri shattered this record by fetching Rs 24.30 crore . If we are to go by the buzz on social media , Om Namo Venkatesaya has smashed both these records and become the highest earner for Nagarjuna .  The buzz claims the makers of Om Namo Venkatesaya have also struck a deal on its international distribution rights for Rs 6 crore . The movie has fetched the highest price for Nagarjuna until now , beating the records of his last releases Soggade Chinni Nayana and Oopiri , which earned Rs 0.65 crore and Rs 4 crore , respectively .  Its theatrical rights for other regions have also fetched record prices . As per the buzz , Om Namo Venkatesaya has earned Rs 35 crore from the sale of its worldwide distribution rights . It is not only the highest price for a Nagarjuna movie , but also a devotional film and the success rate of the King star is the reason behind such a high price .  Om Namo Venkatesaya is a devotional and biographical film , based on the life of Hathiram Baba . Nagarjuna Akkineni is playing the lead role of Baba in the film , while Anushka Shetty and Saurabh Raj Jain appear as Krishnamma and Lord Venkateswara , respectively .
