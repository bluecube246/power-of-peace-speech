


st. albert rcmp arrest suspect in gas station robbery  st. albert rcmp have arrested and charged a suspect after a robbery at a gas station , last week .  police say just before 8:30p.m. , aug. 18 , a man was holding a knife as he walked into an esso gas station , on giroux road and st.albert trail . the suspect demanded car keys from a resident inside the station , but the suspect was unsuccessful and fled the scene .  the next day , about 1 a.m. , police got reports of a suspicious man sleeping in the lobby of the citadel mews care centre in st. albert . the man fit the description of the suspect from the gas station robbery .  the with the help of police dogs , the man was arrested and taken into custody .
