


<h> St. Albert RCMP arrest suspect in gas station robbery <p> St. Albert RCMP have arrested and charged a suspect after a robbery at a gas station , last week . <p> Police say just before 8:30p.m. , Aug. 18 , a man was holding a knife as he walked into an Esso gas station , on Giroux Road and St.Albert Trail . The suspect demanded car keys from a resident inside the station , but the suspect was unsuccessful and fled the scene . <p> The next day , about 1 a.m. , Police got reports of a suspicious man sleeping in the lobby of the Citadel Mews Care Centre in St. Albert . The man fit the description of the suspect from the gas station robbery . <p> The with the help of Police Dogs , the man was arrested and taken into custody .
