


Sensex rallies 181 points on OPEC deal , F&amp;O expiry  Market benchmark Sensex surged over 181 points and the NSE Nifty reclaimed the 8,800-mark on Thursday after global markets cheered a surprise deal by OPEC to cut oil production for the first time in eight years .  PTI Sep 29 , 2016 , 10.12 AM IST  MUMBAI : Market benchmark Sensex surged over 181 points and the NSE Nifty reclaimed the 8,800-mark on Thursday after global markets cheered a surprise deal by OPEC to cut oil production for the first time in eight years .  Short-covering of bets with Thursday being the last session of September expiry in the derivatives segment kept the momentum on .  The 30-share index , which had gained 69.11 points in the previous session , rallied by 181.31 points , or 0.64 per cent , at 28,474.12 .  Oil and gas , realty , PSU , auto and metal stocks were in a sweet spot , which gave the sentiment a lift .  The NSE index Nifty was also trading higher 55.50 points , or the mood was markedly buoyant , bolstered by a flurry of buying following a firming trend in global markets after a surprise deal by OPEC to slash oil output and lift prices .
