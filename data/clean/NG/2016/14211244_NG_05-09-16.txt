Ahmed commiserates with Abiara
http://thenationonlineng.net/ahmed-commiserates-abiara/

<h> Ahmed commiserates with Abiara <p> KIwara State Governor Abdulfatah Ahmed has commiserated with the General Evangelist , Christ Apostolic Church ( CAC ) Worldwide , Evangelist Samuel Kayode Abiara , on the demise of his wife Evangelist Christiana . <p> Ahmed , in a condolence message , described Mrs. Abiara as a woman of virtue , who lived her life with the fear of God and service to humanity . <p> According to the governor , the prayers of the deceased would be missed greatly . <p> He added that the country is in need of prayer warriors to sail out of this difficult economic period . <p> Ahmed prayed God to grant the family the fortitude to bear the loss and the deceased eternal rest .
