Ahmed commiserates with Abiara  KIwara State Governor Abdulfatah Ahmed has commiserated with the General Evangelist , Christ Apostolic Church ( CAC ) Worldwide , Evangelist Samuel Kayode Abiara , on the demise of his wife Evangelist Christiana .  Ahmed , in a condolence message , described Mrs. Abiara as a woman of virtue , who lived her life with the fear of God and service to humanity .  According to the governor , the prayers of the deceased would be missed greatly .  He added that the country is in need of prayer warriors to sail out of this difficult economic period .  Ahmed prayed God to grant the family the fortitude to bear the loss and the deceased eternal rest .
