<h> Steve Winwood Plots Deluxe ' Arc of a Diver ' Release <p> Steve Winwood 's classic 1980 record , ' Arc of a Diver , ' is getting the deluxe reissue . The Second Disc reports that the updated version will come with a bonus disc that includes a number of extra goodies . <p> The updated release , due Sept. 24 in the U.K. , will include three additional tracks . The edited U.S. single version of the ' Arc of a Diver ' title track , a 2010 edit of ' Spanish Dancer , ' and the U.K. 12-inch instrumental version of ' Night Train ' are all new extras for the release . Plus , the fourth track on the bonus disc is a BBC Radio 2 documentary entitled ' Arc of a Diver : The Steve Winwood Story .
