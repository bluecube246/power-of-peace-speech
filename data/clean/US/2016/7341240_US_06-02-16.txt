share this :  the tampa bay buccaneers received a lot of criticism for selecting florida state quarterback jameis winston with the no. 1 pick in the 2015 nfl draft . after one season , it appears they made a smart decision .  winston won the 2016 pepsi nfl rookie of the year award saturday , beating out other top candidates such as st. louis rams running back todd gurley and oakland raiders wide receiver amari cooper .  winston started all 16 games for the buccaneers this season . he threw for a rookie-leading 4,042 yards with 22 touchdown passes and 15 interceptions .  tampa bay finished 6-10 , good for last place in the nfc south . that said , the bucs did improve by four wins from 2014 and their offense , led by winston , running back doug martin and rising star wide receiver mike evans , is on track to be one of the league 's best in a few years .  the 2015 awards will be presented during nfl honors , which airs saturday on cbs at 9 @ @ @ @
