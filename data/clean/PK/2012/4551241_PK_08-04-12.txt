<p> AJMER : Pakistan President Asif Ali Zardari and his son PPP chairman Bilawal Bhutto Zardari on Sunday evening offered prayers at the shrine of Sufi Saint Khwaja Moinuddin Chishti in Ajmer Shareef . President Asif Ali Zardari also donated $1 million to the holy shrine of Khwaja Moinuddin Chishti , Indian media reported . <p> Both President Zardari and his son Bilawal Bhutto laid ' chadars ' at the Mazar . <p> " The soulful happiness that I have experienced at this holy place is beyond explanation . I pray to Allah to make life easy for the entire humanity , " President Zardari wrote in a diary at the shrine . <p> He landed in the city in a chopper soon after 4 pm on Sunday after a lunch with Prime Minister Manmohan Singh and several other leaders in New Delhi . <p> Accompanying the president in his Ajmer visit was his 23-year-old son Bilawal Bhutto Zardari . Zardari last visited Ajmer in 2005 with his wife Benazir Bhutto .
