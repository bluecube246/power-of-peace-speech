Apple accused of $71 million tax evasion in China
http://tribune.com.pk/story/954412/apple-accused-over-71-million-in-chinese-taxes/

<p> BEIJING : A Chinese subsidiary of US tech giant Apple failed to pay 452 million yuan in taxes due at the end of 2013 , Beijing 's finance ministry said . <p> Apple has already paid the back taxes , along with late fees totalling 65 million yuan , the ministry said on its website . <p> California-based Apple 's products are hugely popular in China and the country is increasingly one of its most important markets . <p> Apple 's infraction -- it included maintenance costs in pretax deductions -- was detailed as part of a larger report posted Wednesday on finance ministry inspections into a variety of Chinese and foreign companies . <p> The company also understated costs of 3.45 billion yuan , the report said , along with unspecified " violation issues " . <p> Apple representatives in China could not immediately be reached for comment . <p> Long queues of buyers have often besieged Apple outlets in the country when a new gadget is launched . <p> But it has sometimes been targeted by state media , with vehement attacks on its customer @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ chief executive Tim Cook . <p> Apple 's iPhone sales have surged 85 per cent in Greater China -- which includes Hong Kong and Taiwan -- with revenue from the region more than doubling to $13 billion for the latest quarter ended June 27 , according to the company .
