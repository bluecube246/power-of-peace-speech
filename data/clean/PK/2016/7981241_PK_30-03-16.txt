Wheat procurement begins in Sindh
http://www.brecorder.com/pakistan/markets/287730-wheat-procurement-begins-in-sindh.html

wheat procurement begins in sindh  karachi : the procurement of wheat from farmers at the rate of rs. 1,300 per mound has been started in sindh province from wednesday ( march 30 ) .  director food sindh muhammad bachal told app here on wednesday that wheat procurement target has been set at 11,00,000 metric tonne this year , which is 200,000 metric tonnes more than that of the previous year .  he said that the provincial government is making all possible efforts to provide maximum relief to farmers to bring prosperity in their lives .  the director food informed that 442 procurement centres have been set up in the province , while gunny bags have also been provided to farmers .  however , he added , no procurement centre has been established in karachi and its adjoining areas as the crop has not been grown in these areas .  muhammad bachal said that monitoring team have been formed at district level to ensure transparency in procurement process and promptly address any complaints by farmers .
