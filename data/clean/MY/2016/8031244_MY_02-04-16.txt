Anti-GST Rally Set To Take Place This Afternoon
http://www.malaysiandigest.com/frontpage/29-4-tile/603776-anti-gst-rally-set-to-take-place-this-afternoon.html

<p> Despite police warning , the Anti Goods and Services Tax ( GST ) rally is set to take place this afternoon . <p> The rally , organised by Parti Amanah Negara ( PAN ) together with PKR and DAP is to protest the implementation of GST , which marked its first year of anniversary yesterday . <p> About 50,000 people are expected to gather for the rally which will begin from Sogo shopping complex . The rally goers will then proceed to Dataran Merdeka . <p> The anti-GST secretariat had also confirmed that the rally will be attended by former Prime Minister Tun Dr Mahathir Mohamad . <p> Police had on Thursday said that they have not received all the necessary documents from the rally organisers . <p> Dang Wangi police chief ACP Zainol Samah said since they have not received all the relevant documents , the notice was not able to be processed as per the Fourth Schedule of the Peaceful Assembly Act ( PAA ) 2012 . <p> " In the first meeting with the organisers on March 23 , they had been informed to @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ of now , no complete documents have been received . <p> " Besides that , the Kuala Lumpur City Hall has also informed the police that on Saturday , Dataran Merdeka can not be used as there are preparations involved for the car free morning programme scheduled the next day , " he was quoted by Bernama.He also warned that stern action against any individuals who violated the law as stipulated under Section 11 of PAA 2012 . <p> He advised the organisers to look for alternative locations such as Padang Merbok or the Dewan Sultan Sulaiman Club field .
