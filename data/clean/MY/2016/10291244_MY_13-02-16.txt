ROS Lboard  Ku Nan : Govt committed to building affordable houses in the Federal Territories  February 13 , 2016  He expects 55,000 units of affordable houses would be built by end of the year with some units ready now .  KUALA LUMPUR : The government is committed to building 80,000 units of affordable houses in the Federal Territories of Kuala Lumpur , Putrajaya and Labuan by 2018 , said Federal Territories Minister Tengku Adnan Tengku Mansor .  He said construction of more than 36,000 units of the houses had taken off the ground since two years ago , with some of the units now ready .  " The government is always concerned and understand the people 's need for affordable houses in the city .  " We will not break our promise because we want to see the people live in good surroundings , " he said in his speech at the " Kenduri 1Wilayah Persekutuan " event in Wangsa Maju , here , today .
