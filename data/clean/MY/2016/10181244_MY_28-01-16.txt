IGP, Ismail Sabri apply to strike out Nurul Izzah's suit

<h> ROS Lboard <h> IGP , Ismail Sabri apply to strike out Nurul Izzah 's suit <p> January 28 , 2016 <p> Nurul Izzah will submit an objection to the Attorney-General 's Chambers against the IGP 's and Ismail Sabri 's appeal . <p> KUALA LUMPUR : Inspector-General of Police Khalid Abu Bakar and Rural and Regional Development Minister Ismail Sabri Yaakob have filed an application to strike out a suit by PKR vice-president Nurul Izzah Anwar relating to allegations that she was a traitor to the country . <p> This matter was disclosed by counsel N Surendran and Latheefa Koya , who represented Nurul Izzah , when met by reporters after case management before High Court Judge Nor Bee Ariffin today . <p> The proceeding in chambers was also attended by Federal Counsel Shaiful Nizam Shahrin , representing Khalid and Ismail Sabri who were named as defendants in the suit . <p> Surendran said both defendants filed their application to strike out the suit yesterday . <p> Meanwhile , Latheefa said her client would submit an objection against the Attorney-General 's Chambers that represented the two defendants @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ both defendants are being sued in their personal capacity and should not be represented by the Attorney-General as our client did not name the government of Malaysia in her suit , " she said . <p> Nurul Izzah , who is also Lembah Pantai Member of Parliament , filed the suit on November 26 , 2015 . She alleged that Khalid had uttered slanderous words against her during a media conference at the Bukit Aman police headquarters on November 22 , 2015 . <p> In her suit , Nurul Izzah said the libellous statements were issued several days after her photograph with Jacel Kiram , the daughter of Jamalulail Kiram who was said to have ordered and led the intrusion in Sabah in 2013 , was published . <p> Nurul Izzah alleged it had portrayed her as a traitor to the country and the mastermind behind the Sulu intrusion in Sabah . <p> She also alleged that on the same day ( November 22 , 2015 ) , Ismail Sabri also issued slanderous words in a media conference at an event in Bera , Pahang that implied she was a traitor @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ di-Pertuan Agong . <p> She said the statements by the two defendants were published and broadcast in both print and electronic media , and claimed that due to the libellous statements , she suffered serious damage to her reputation as a Parliamentarian and politician . <p> Hence , she is seeking general damages , aggravated damages , benefits , costs and other relief deemed appropriate by the court . <p> -- BERNAMA <h> Comments <p> Readers are required to have a valid Facebook account to comment on this story .
