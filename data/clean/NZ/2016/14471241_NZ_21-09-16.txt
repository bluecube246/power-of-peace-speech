<h> Search ends in Thai boat accident after 28 bodies recovered <p> BANGKOK ( AP ) " Thai officials have ended the search for victims of a weekend boat accident after recovering 28 bodies and accounting for all the people listed as missing . <p> The Marine Department announced Wednesday that the last body recovered from Sunday 's accident on the Chao Phraya river was that of a 70-year-old woman . <p> After her body was recovered Tuesday , the damaged boat was lifted from the water for an inspection to investigate the cause of the accident . <p> The boat was carrying more than 100 Thais home from an Islamic religious festival when it hit an underwater obstruction and sank quickly just a few meters ( yards ) from shore , about 80 kilometers ( 50 miles ) north of Bangkok .
