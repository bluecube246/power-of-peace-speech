Cameroon leader pardons French lawyer jailed for graft

<h> Related Links <p> Yaound ? -- Cameroonian President Paul Biya on Monday pardoned a French lawyer serving a 25-year sentence for stealing 1.5 million euros ( R24,6m ) from public funds . <p> Biya said in a statement read on state radio that Lydienne Yen-Ayoum , a Cameroonian who acquired French nationality after marrying a Frenchman , would no longer have to serve the remainder of her sentence . <p> Arrested in January 2008 , she was sentenced to 25 years in jail in September 2014 and has so far spent eight years in prison . <p> The Supreme Court last year upheld the sentence but Yen-Ayoum has always proclaimed her innocence . <p> " It 's a miracle . She is being freed from prison this evening , " her lawyer Caroline Wassermann said in Paris . <p> Yen-Ayoum was arrested along with several others in an anti-corruption sweep .
