<h> Calls to discipline SA ambassadors to Singapore , Israel <p> JOHANNESBURG -- The Chairperson of the Portfolio Committee on International Relations and Cooperation , Siphosezwe Masango , said Minister Maite Nkoana-Mashabane should investigate allegations against South Africa 's ambassadors to Singapore and Israel . <p> " The Committee received a briefing from the Department of International Relations and Cooperation on , among other things , allegations reported in the media against South Africa 's High Commissioner to Singapore , Ms Houley Ngubeni , and the Ambassador to Israel , Mr Sisa Ngombane , " the committee said in a statement . <p> Masango said it was important that the record of those who represent South Africa abroad is above reproach . <p> " South Africa 's image should not be compromised by the same people we send abroad to promote our country . We hope that the Minister will take appropriate corrective actions should the allegations be true , " he said . <p> The committee said it was informed that Ngubeni 's security clearance certificate had been withdrawn .
