Irving, Barnes to complete US Olympic team
http://www.supersport.com/basketball/nba/news/160626/Irving_Barnes_to_complete_US_Olympic_team

irving , barnes to complete us olympic team  cleveland cavaliers guard kyrie irving and golden state forward harrison barnes have agreed to play for the united states at the rio olympics , completing the american roster , espn reported on saturday .  citing unidentified sources , the us-based sport 's network 's website said irving , who helped the cavaliers win their first nba crown last weekend , and barnes , whose warriors were on the losing end in the finals , agreed to the brazil trip saturday .  the us squad will be favoured to win a third consecutive olympic gold medal in rio under coach mike krzyzewski , who will leave after the games following a decade in the job . gregg popovich of the san antonio spurs is set to replace him after rio .  the americans will stage a training camp in las vegas on july 18-21 then play five warm-up games ahead of the august 6 start of the men 's olympic tournament .
