<h> Philex confirms feasibility of $1.5bn Silangan project in the Philippines <p> 30 October 2014 <p> A study undertaken by gold and copper producer Philex Mining has confirmed the viability of its $1.5bn Silangan project on the island of Mindanao in southern Philippines . <p> Silangan has estimated reserves of five billion pounds of copper and nine million ounces of gold . <p> The mine is expected to begin production in 2018 and become a key revenue generator for Philex after its Padcal mine closes in 2020 , reported Reuters . <p> Announcing the third quarter results , Philex chairman Manuel Pangilinan said that in the next two months the company will focus on the next big prospective mine , the Silangan project , and finding additional reserves to extend the life of Padcal mine . <p> " Philex plans to invest up to PHP4bn ( $89m ) in the Silangan project this year . " <p> " We expect the DFS to be completed by mid-next year and are also looking forward to secure the remaining regulatory approvals to commence production .
