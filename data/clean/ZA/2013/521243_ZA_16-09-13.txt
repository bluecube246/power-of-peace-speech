Lion kills Ethiopian zookeeper

<h> Related Links <p> Addis Ababa - A lion at the Addis Ababa zoo attacked and killed a zookeeper on Monday after he forgot to close the door to the inner cage where the lion sleeps , officials said . <p> The lion , named Kenenisa after a famous Ethiopian runner , killed Abera Silsay , 51 , as he was cleaning the cage . <p> " He entered cage number 10 where Kenenisa lives and he forgot to close the door to the lion 's sleeping chamber ... Finally , the lion came and he mauled him , " the zoo 's director general Musie Kiflom told AFP . <p> The lion bit Abera 's neck as guards tried to scare Kenenisa off by shooting live rounds into the air and pounding on the cage 's roof . <p> It is the second time a zookeeper has been killed by a lion at the centre in the past 17 years . <p> Musie said the zoo will step up its training for keepers after the attack . <p> " We have to update them on how to @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ , how to keep them , how to clean them , " he said speaking near the cage where the attack took place . <p> Kenenisa the lion was unharmed and remains in the zoo , which was closed on Monday after the incident . <p> The Addis Ababa zoo is home to 15 endangered Abyssinian Lions , found in Ethiopia and famous for their black manes . <p> The lions are kept in enclosed cages , but the zoo is set to move to a larger , grassier enclosure in the Ethiopian capital in the next 13 months . <p> The Addis Ababa zoo was opened in 1948 by former emperor Haile Selassie , who established the centre to house his pet cub lions Molla and Lullu . <p> 24. com publishes all comments posted on articles provided that they adhere to our Comments Policy .
