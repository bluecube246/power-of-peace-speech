<p> A majority of graduates from National University of Singapore , Nanyang Technological University and Singapore Management University have managed to find employment within six months of graduating . <p> This is according to results from the first joint graduate employment survey conducted by the three universities which surveyed a total of 9,668 full-time , fresh graduates . <p> As of Nov 1 last year , 91 per cent of graduates managed to secure a job , with 85.6 per cent of them employed in full-time permanent jobs . <p> The median gross monthly salary among fresh graduates employed in full-time permanent employment was $3,050 , while the average gross monthly salary was $3,260 .
