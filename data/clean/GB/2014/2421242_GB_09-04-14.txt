<h> New members of Invest NI unveiled <p> ENTERPRISE Minister Arlene Foster has announced seven new appointments to the board of Invest NI . <p> The appointments of Deborah Lange , Mark Nodder and Mark Sweeney are for five years with Padraig Canavan , Brian Baird , Peter McNaney and Judith Totten appointed for three . The minister said : " The board of Invest NI has an important role in the development of our plans to create sustainable growth , jobs and prosperity . I look forward to working with the new members who bring with them a strong mix of business talent , insight and experience that will assist in the development of Invest NI 's business and strategic plans over the next few years .
