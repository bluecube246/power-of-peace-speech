Chairman of Southern Health NHS Foundation Trust resigns
http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-england-36163375

' Significant scrutiny '  A report commissioned by NHS England , published in December 2015 , discovered that only 272 of the 722 deaths in the trust over the previous four years were investigated .  The trust was issued a warning notice by the CQC following an inspection in January .  Image caption Mr Petter said he was leaving the trust ' with great sadness '  In a statement Mr Petter said : " The trust has recently undergone a significant amount of scrutiny in some service areas and , given the challenges it faces , I feel it is appropriate for me to allow new board leadership to take forward the improvements .  " I have been involved in Southern Health since the trust was formed in 2011 as a non-executive director and since August 2015 as chairman .  " It is with great sadness that I now leave the organisation .  " I offer my support and best wishes to all our staff whose dedication and commitment I am continuously impressed by . "
