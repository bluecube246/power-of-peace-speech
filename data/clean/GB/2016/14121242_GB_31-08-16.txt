theresa may addresses cabinet at start of brexit meeting  31 august 2016 last updated at 12:13 bst  prime minister theresa may tells the cabinet that they have the opportunity " to forge a new positive role for the uk in the world " as she starts a special cabinet meeting at her official chequers country residence .  the meeting of the cabinet is the first since the summer recess and is focusing on the way forward for the uk on leaving the european union .
